Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

How long does it take?

A typical build takes 7-14 days. The free visibility scan and strategy call happen before we start, usually within the first week of contact.

I already have a Google Business Profile - can you optimize it?

Yes. Most established businesses have one, but it's incomplete or set up wrong. We clean it up end-to-end: full service list, accurate service area, photos refreshed, posts automated, and category mapping verified.

I already have a website - why would I need a new one?

We don't push new builds when an existing site is fixable. But many DIY and template sites aren't structured for local SEO - no dedicated service pages, slow load times, JavaScript-rendered content that crawlers don't read well, or missing schema. After the scan and review, we'll tell you honestly whether your site needs a rebuild or just tuning.

How do you measure results?

We re-run your scan 30-60 days after launch and compare it to the original. The heatmap is your before/after. We also track call volume, Google Business Profile views, and review velocity month-over-month.

What if I'm already paying for SEO?

Many businesses are paying for SEO that isn't local-specific. We can review what they're doing and tell you if there's overlap. Sometimes the existing SEO work is fine and what's missing is the foundation - the site itself and the Google profile.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. Most builds can be split into multiple payments. We'll lay out the options on the strategy call.

What if I don't have professional photos?

We'll work with what you have. For trades and service businesses, we can also point you to affordable local photography or use stock placeholders that can be swapped later.
