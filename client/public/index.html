Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

Most projects take about 2–4 weeks once we start. The free visibility scan and strategy call happen first, usually within your first week of reaching out.

I already have a Google Business Profile — can you optimize it?

Yes. Most established businesses have one, but it's incomplete or set up wrong. We clean it up end-to-end: full service list, accurate service area, refreshed photos, and listing you in the right categories.

I already have a website — why would I need a new one?

We don't push new builds when an existing site is fixable. But many DIY and template sites aren't set up for local search — they load slowly, they're not built the way Google wants for local, or they're missing the behind-the-scenes setup that helps you rank. After the scan and review, we'll tell you honestly whether your site needs a rebuild or just some tuning.

How do you measure results?

We re-run your visibility scan every 30 days and compare it to the original. As your rankings improve, we expand the scan radius so you can see how far your visibility has grown.

What if I'm already paying for SEO?

Many businesses are paying for SEO that isn't local-specific. We can review what they're doing and tell you if there's overlap. Sometimes the existing SEO work is fine and what's missing is the foundation — the site itself and the Google profile.

What if I don't have professional photos?

We'll work with what you have. If your existing photos need work, we can clean them up significantly — and in many cases, help you get quality images that make your business look its best.
