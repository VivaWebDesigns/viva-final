What the Scan Shows

One Search From Your Office Does Not Show the Full Picture

Google's local results change depending on where the person searching is located. Your business might rank well from one street and disappear only a few miles away.

A visibility scan tests searches from multiple locations throughout your service area so you can see the market the way potential customers actually experience it.

How to Read the Results

Your Ranking Changes Across the Market

  • Rankings 1–3 indicate strong local visibility.
  • Rankings 4–10 mean stronger competitors may appear before the business.
  • Rankings above 10 become increasingly difficult for customers to discover.
  • “20+” means the business was outside the first 20 local results from that location.

The goal is not merely to rank well at your business address. It is to remain visible throughout the areas where your customers live and work.
