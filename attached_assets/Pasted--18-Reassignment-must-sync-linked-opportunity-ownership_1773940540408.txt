#18 - Reassignment must sync linked opportunity ownership

What & Why
After Task #17, lead ownership restrictions are working, but a new
mismatch exists between CRM lead visibility and Sales Pipeline
visibility.

Observed behavior:
- A lead reassigned to a sales rep appears correctly in the CRM
  lead list for that rep
- The same user cannot see the linked record in Sales Pipeline
- This strongly suggests crmLeads.assignedTo is updated, but the
  linked opportunity.assignedTo is not

This is not a permissions redesign task. Ownership filtering should
remain intact. The likely issue is data sync between lead ownership
and opportunity ownership.

Do NOT implement yet. Plan mode only.

Analyze the current system end-to-end.

Focus on:
- crmLeads.assignedTo as the canonical lead ownership field
- opportunity assignedTo field
- relationship between leads and opportunities (leadId or equivalent)
- bulk assign route (/api/crm/leads/bulk/assign)
- single lead reassignment path (PUT /api/crm/leads/:id or equivalent)
- lead detail reassignment flow
- lead creation route (/api/crm/leads/manual)
- opportunity creation/inheritance logic
- pipeline list and board filtering logic
- whether reassignment currently updates only the lead, or also the
  linked opportunity/opportunities

Questions to answer:
1. When a lead is reassigned, does the linked opportunity also get
   reassigned?
2. If not, exactly where is that gap occurring?
3. Is pipeline filtering based solely on opportunity.assignedTo?
4. Are the missing pipeline records caused by stale or null
   opportunity ownership?
5. Can one lead have multiple linked opportunities, and if so how
   should reassignment behave?
6. What should be the source of truth for ownership after a lead
   already has a linked opportunity?

Desired direction:
- Keep lead ownership as the source of truth
- Keep existing strict access control from Task #17
- Do not loosen pipeline filtering
- Do not create a second ownership system
- Reassignment of a lead should keep linked opportunity ownership
  in sync

Output:
1. Root cause explanation
2. Exact routes/functions where ownership sync is breaking
3. Recommended implementation plan
4. Files that need to change
5. Any edge cases or risks
