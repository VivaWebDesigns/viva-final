FEATURE SUMMARY
Admins configure task templates by destination pipeline stage:
- New Lead
- Contacted
- Demo Scheduled
- Demo Completed
- Payment Sent
- Closed-Won
- Closed-Lost

When a lead moves into a supported stage, active templates for that stage generate real tasks attached to the lead/client profile Tasks tab.
Admins and sales reps can see the generated tasks.
Admins manage the configuration.
