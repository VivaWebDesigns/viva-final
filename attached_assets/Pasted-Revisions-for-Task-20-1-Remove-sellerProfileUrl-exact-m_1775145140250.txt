sellerProfileUrlSeen, so sellerProfileUrl cannot be used

Unless sellerProfileUrlSeen is later added to the route
payload, do not include it in scoreCaptureMatch().

2. Promote first name + listing title exact match to high
confidence
Update confidence rules:

High confidence:
- exact threadIdentifier match
- OR normalized sellerFirstNameSeen + normalized
  listingTitleSeen exact match

Medium confidence:
- sellerFirstNameSeen + fuzzy / partial listing title
  match
- OR sellerFirstNameSeen + listing title + join year
  where title is imperfect

Low confidence:
- first name only
- listing title only
- facebook join year only
- conflicting signals
- unclear match
