Requirements
- Replace the existing Business Trade text input with a select dropdown.
- Keep Business Trade as a required field.
- Use the canonical values above as the stored values.
- In English mode, show the English labels.
- In Spanish mode, show the Spanish labels.
- Add a placeholder option that is also translated:
  English: Select business trade
  Spanish: Selecciona el giro del negocio
- Do not preselect a real trade by default.
- The form must not submit unless a trade is selected.
- Save the canonical value into the existing Business Trade field.
- Do not store separate English/Spanish text values depending on language.
- Existing records should still display correctly.
- If old records contain plain English text instead of canonical values, make the display layer handle them gracefully rather than breaking.
- Keep styling consistent with the rest of the form.
- Do not change any other lead fields.
- Do not change lead creation logic other than the Business Trade input type and label rendering.

Validation
- In EN mode, the dropdown shows English trade names.
- In ES mode, the dropdown shows Spanish trade names.
- Selecting a trade in either language stores the same canonical value.
- Switching languages changes only the visible labels, not the saved underlying value.
- Business Trade remains required.
- No other form behavior changes.
